Writer & Producer

I'm an independent writer/producer with more than five years of experience writting original soundtracks, scoring media, and producing standalone albums. I started this journey is 2019 and have no plan of stopping. I'm most well known for my upbeat hip-hop/pop production style, built around deep pockets and tight percussion but I am comfortable wokring with any genre. My goal is to make music that puts a production on a pedestal that brings it to new heights.



Sound is essential to the human experience; don't neglect it.

II. Projects

Solitary Moon EP

Released in 2023, the Solitary Moon EP quickly assimilated into the lofi genre. These five tracks were put together to explore loneliness. Each track was arranged and produced by me, mixing early hip-hop percussion with modern synths and production styles.

Goblin Wrangler

Background gameplay music for the mobile puzzle game: Goblin Wrangler. I collaborated with developers to create an arrangment that captured the feel of a goblin-filled dungeon. I built the track to lighthearted yet gritty. It was also designed as a seamless loop for continous gameplay.

Backrooms: Fractured Dimensions

I wrote an encompassing OST for Backrooms: Fracture Dimensions, a chaser horror game where players explore a liminal space while avoiding detection. The goal of these tracks were to amplify the games tone by building an errie atmosphere that keep you on your toes.
